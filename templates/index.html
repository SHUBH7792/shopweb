About SHUBH CHAUHAN POTTERIES

With over 20 years of expertise, Shubh Chauhan Potteries is one of India's leading manufacturers and wholesale suppliers of clay oil lamps (diyas). Renowned for producing designer, handcrafted, and traditional earthen lamps in large volumes, we specialize exclusively in bulk orders for businesses, retailers, and exporters. Our high-quality clay diyas are not only sold across India but also exported in large quantities to international markets, supporting festive and cultural needs worldwide. As one of the biggest diya producers in the industry, we take pride in helping aspiring entrepreneurs and small businesses start their own ventures with minimal and affordable investment. Whether you're looking to start a handicraft business, open a festive retail shop, or simply source clay lamps at wholesale rates, we are your trusted partner in quality, consistency, and scale.
